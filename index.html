About us

information

In the official reptile-club we have the best place for you to buy a reptile and insect. Our reptiles are only bred in captivity. We have locations all around europe. Come visit on of our locations, bring your family too.

There are cafeterias to grab a bite to eat while looking for your new reptile. We also have tons of great terrariums, aqwariums, vivariums, and all other iums. Great decorations, calcium and a wide variety of foods for your pet.
